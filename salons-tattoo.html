Built for how people actually choose a shop

Your site should answer the top questions in under 10 seconds on a phone.

  • Tap-to-call and tap-to-text
  • Tap-for-directions via Google Maps
  • Clear services list (or styles, specialties, pricing ranges)
  • Artist or stylist list with portfolio links
  • Fast-loading gallery that does not choke on weak signal
  • Booking link to your current system (Squarespace, Vagaro, Acuity, Fresha, etc.)

Appointments, deposits, and “hybrid” booking

You do not have to rebuild your booking system to own your website.

I can build an ownership-based main site and route booking buttons to whatever you already use. Customers get a clean storefront you control, while your scheduling and payments stay where they are.
