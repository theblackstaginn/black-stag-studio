About Black Stag Studio

I’m a hands-on builder who cares more about whether your site works than whether it follows the latest design trend.

I work with small businesses, rural shops, and creative brands that are tired of clunky, half-broken websites. You don’t need an agency – you need a site that:

  • Loads quickly on a basic phone signal
  • Makes it obvious what you sell or do
  • Shows your space and work honestly
  • Gets people to call, visit, or buy

Everything I build is custom-coded for your business. No drag-and-drop templates, no locked-in website builders, and no monthly “design retainers” just to keep the lights on.

Your domain and hosting go in your name. You get clean HTML/CSS, real image handling, and a layout that feels like your actual business. After launch, updates are simple: $60/hr when you need changes, and nothing when you don’t.
